Singer, Actress and Model: Suzy Bae

About Suzy Bae

  • 1994: Born in Buk Distric, Gwangju, South Korea.
  • 2009-2010: Debuted as a member of a K-pop music group called Miss A from JYP Entertainment.
  • 2010: Became a fixed host for MBC Show! Music Core.
  • 2011: Debuted as an actress in the music teen drama Dream High.
  • 2012: She made her film debut with Architecture 101 and she was the first Korean Female celebrity who won several of rookie awards like a singer rookie award, drama rookie award and film rookie awards.
  • 2013: Graduated from School Performing Arts Seoul and starred in a historical fusion drama Gu Family Book where she gained acting recognition and won the Top Excellence award.
  • 2014: Casted in a film called The Sound of a Flower which was released in 2015 and in the same year she collapped with a taiwanese singer-actor Show Lo in a single "Together In Love" featured in his almum Reality Show.
  • 2016: Released a single called "Dream" and won the "Best Collaboration" award at the Mnet Asian Music Awards. She also starred in a romance melodrama Uncontrollably Fond. At the same time she released two OSTs for the drama including a self-composed song. She is the first Korean female celebrity to have a wax figure of herself displayed at Madama Tussauds Hongkong.
  • 2017: Debutd as a solo artist.
  • 2019: Left JYP Entertainment as her contract expired and signed a new contract with an acting agency Management SOOP.
  • 2022: She made her comback as a singer after 4 years with the song "Satellite".
